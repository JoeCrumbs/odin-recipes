Fried Chicken

Description

This is the ultimate crispy fried chicken recipe. Why? Because it uses all the best techniques to guarantee the juiciest, most flavorful, and crispiest chicken, every single time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • Spices and seasonings (paprika, salt, and pepper)
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Make the Breading
  2. Dredge the Chicken
  3. Fry the Chicken
