Cajun Chicken and Sausage Jambalaya

Description

This Cajun chicken and sausage jambalaya is incredibly flavorful. It's a timeless one-pot comfort food that's great for any occasion, and it is especially perfect for celebrating Mardi Gras.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 1/4 pound andouille smoked sausage, sliced into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3/4 pound ground pork
  • 1 large yellow or white onion, diced
  • And more

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Prepare the ingredients.
  3. Mix them together.
  4. Serve the finished dish!
