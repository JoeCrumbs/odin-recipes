Lasagna

Description

Preparing lasagna can take some time, but the end result is definitely worth it. The recipe below includes a full list of ingredients and detailed instructions, but let's start by covering the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat (ground beef and sweet italian sausage)
  • Onion and garlic
  • Various tomatoe products
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings (fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper)
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese (Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta)
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
